About the Project JOstainable

JOstainable is a Jordanian e-commerce leader that connects local communities buyers and sellers in Jordan. We exist to enable economic opportunity for individuals, entrepreneurs, small businesses and organizations. Our vision is manifested in the JOstainable to enable individuals and local communities to achieve profit by reusing and recycling for goods and products, in order to have an opprotunity to sell them again using our platform. The JOstainable team members are not all Jordan based. The site's vision to be a global workforce provides a unique understanding of the benefits of the recycling and reusing of the products, and it effects on the sustainable developments goals.
